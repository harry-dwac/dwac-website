Digital World Arbitration Centre — Model Arbitration Clauses

Introduction

The following model clauses are recommended for inclusion in contracts, platform terms of service, smart contracts, and other agreements where parties wish to designate DWAC as the arbitral institution. Select the clause type appropriate for your use case.

Clause Type 1: Standard Arbitration Clause / 标准仲裁条款

EN: Any dispute arising from or in connection with this agreement shall be submitted to the Digital World Arbitration Centre (DWAC) for arbitration in accordance with the DWAC Arbitration Rules then in effect. The arbitral award shall be final and binding on both parties.

中文: 凡因本协议引起的或与本协议有关的一切争议，均应提交数字世界仲裁中心（DWAC），按照申请仲裁时该中心现行有效的仲裁规则进行仲裁。仲裁裁决是终局的，对双方均有约束力。

Clause Type 2: Smart Contract Embedded Clause / 智能合约嵌入条款

EN: The parties agree that any dispute arising from the execution or interpretation of this smart contract (Contract Address: [________________________] on [________________________] blockchain) shall be resolved through arbitration administered by the Digital World Arbitration Centre (DWAC). The smart contract shall include a dispute flag mechanism that, when triggered, pauses the contested execution pending resolution. The parties agree that blockchain transaction records, contract state data, and event logs shall constitute admissible evidence.

中文: 双方同意，因本智能合约（合约地址: [________________________]，区块链: [________________________]）的执行或解释引起的任何争议，应通过数字世界仲裁中心（DWAC）管理的仲裁解决。智能合约应包含争议标记机制，触发时暂停争议执行的待决事项。双方同意，区块链交易记录、合约状态数据和事件日志构成可采信证据。

Clause Type 3: Crypto Transaction Arbitration Clause / 加密货币交易仲裁条款

EN: Any dispute arising from cryptocurrency transactions between the parties, including but not limited to failed transfers, unauthorized transactions, wallet access disputes, and token classification disputes, shall be submitted to the Digital World Arbitration Centre (DWAC) for arbitration. The parties agree that on-chain transaction data verified by blockchain explorers shall constitute prima facie evidence. The arbitral tribunal may order interim measures including token freezing, transaction reversal (where technically feasible), or smart contract pause.

中文: 双方之间因加密货币交易引起的任何争议，包括但不限于转账失败、未授权交易、钱包访问争议和代币分类争议，应提交数字世界仲裁中心（DWAC）仲裁。双方同意，经区块链浏览器验证的链上交易数据构成初步证据。仲裁庭可下令采取临时措施，包括代币冻结、交易回滚（技术可行时）或智能合约暂停。

Clause Type 4: AI Service Arbitration Clause / AI 服务仲裁条款

EN: Any dispute arising from AI-generated outputs, AI service performance, algorithmic bias claims, or AI Agent interactions shall be submitted to the Digital World Arbitration Centre (DWAC) for arbitration. The parties agree that AI interaction logs, model version records, training data descriptions (where available), and output audit trails shall constitute admissible evidence. Where an AI Agent acts on behalf of a party, the principal party shall be bound by the Agent's actions within the scope of its authorization.

中文: 因 AI 生成的输出、AI 服务性能、算法偏见主张或 AI 代理交互引起的任何争议，应提交数字世界仲裁中心（DWAC）仲裁。双方同意，AI 交互日志、模型版本记录、训练数据描述（如可获得）和输出审计记录构成可采信证据。当 AI 代理代表一方当事人行事时，委托方应受其代理在授权范围内行为的约束。

Clause Type 5: DAO Governance Arbitration Clause / DAO 治理仲裁条款

EN: Any dispute arising from DAO governance actions, including but not limited to proposal execution disputes, token voting irregularities, treasury management disputes, and smart contract upgrade conflicts, shall be submitted to the Digital World Arbitration Centre (DWAC) for arbitration. The parties acknowledge that on-chain governance records, voting snapshots, and proposal execution logs shall constitute admissible evidence. The arbitral tribunal may order DAO governance actions to be suspended pending resolution.

中文: 因 DAO 治理行动引起的任何争议，包括但不限于提案执行争议、代币投票异常、金库管理争议和智能合约升级冲突，应提交数字世界仲裁中心（DWAC）仲裁。双方确认，链上治理记录、投票快照和提案执行日志构成可采信证据。仲裁庭可命令暂停 DAO 治理行动待争议解决。

Clause Type 6: Platform User Agreement Arbitration Clause / 平台用户协议仲裁条款

EN: Any dispute between the User and the Platform arising from the use of the Platform's services, including but not limited to account suspension, content moderation, data processing, and digital asset custody, shall be submitted to the Digital World Arbitration Centre (DWAC) for arbitration. The User and the Platform agree that server logs, user activity records, and platform terms snapshots shall constitute admissible evidence. This clause constitutes an arbitration agreement between the User and the Platform upon the User's registration or continued use of the Platform.

中文: 用户与平台之间因使用平台服务引起的任何争议，包括但不限于账户暂停、内容审核、数据处理和数字资产托管，应提交数字世界仲裁中心（DWAC）仲裁。用户和平台同意，服务器日志、用户活动记录和平台条款快照构成可采信证据。本条款在用户注册或继续使用平台时构成用户与平台之间的仲裁协议。

Clause Type 7: Cross-Border Digital Commerce Arbitration Clause / 跨境数字商务仲裁条款

EN: Any dispute arising from cross-border digital commerce, including but not limited to digital goods delivery, cross-border payment disputes, digital service level agreements, and regulatory compliance conflicts across jurisdictions, shall be submitted to the Digital World Arbitration Centre (DWAC) for arbitration. The parties agree that the arbitration shall be conducted in accordance with the DWAC Arbitration Rules. The place of arbitration shall be [________________________] unless otherwise agreed. The language of arbitration shall be [English / Chinese / Bilingual]. The arbitral award shall be recognized and enforced under the New York Convention (1958) and applicable local laws.

中文: 因跨境数字商务引起的任何争议，包括但不限于数字商品交付、跨境支付争议、数字服务级别协议和跨司法管辖区监管合规冲突，应提交数字世界仲裁中心（DWAC）仲裁。双方同意，仲裁应按照 DWAC 仲裁规则进行。仲裁地为 [________________________]，另有约定除外。仲裁语言为 [英文/中文/双语]。仲裁裁决应依照《纽约公约》（1958年）和适用当地法律予以承认和执行。

Optional Addendum: Multi-Tier Dispute Resolution / 可选附录：多层次争议解决

EN: Before submitting any dispute to DWAC arbitration, the parties shall attempt in good faith to resolve the dispute through:

1. Direct negotiation for a period of [__] days;

2. If unresolved, mediation administered by DWAC for a period of [__] days;

3. If still unresolved, arbitration administered by DWAC in accordance with the DWAC Arbitration Rules.

中文: 在将任何争议提交 DWAC 仲裁之前，双方应善意尝试通过以下方式解决争议：

1. 直接协商，期限为 [__] 天；

2. 如未解决，由 DWAC 主持调解，期限为 [__] 天；

3. 如仍未解决，按照 DWAC 仲裁规则由 DWAC 仲裁。

Usage Notes / 使用说明

1. Model clauses may be adapted to specific contractual contexts

2. Parties are encouraged to specify the number of arbitrators, language, and place of arbitration

3. Smart contract embedded clauses should be reviewed by technical and legal counsel

4. DWAC recommends including the multi-tier dispute resolution addendum for complex disputes

5. For the latest version of model clauses, visit dwac.net/resources/forms/

1. 示范条款可根据特定合同场景进行调整

2. 鼓励当事人明确仲裁员人数、仲裁语言和仲裁地

3. 智能合约嵌入条款应由技术和法律顾问审阅

4. DWAC 建议对复杂争议附设多层次争议解决附录

5. 获取最新版本示范条款，请访问 dwac.net/resources/forms/

# DWAC Arbitration Forms & Templates / DWAC 仲裁文书模板库

Overview / 概述

This repository contains the official arbitration forms and templates of the Digital World Arbitration Centre (DWAC). All templates are bilingual (English/Chinese) and specifically adapted for digital world disputes, including blockchain evidence, AI Agent representation, smart contract disputes, and digital asset preservation.

本仓库包含数字世界仲裁中心（DWAC）的官方仲裁文书模板。所有模板均为双语（英文/中文），并专门适配数字世界争议，包括区块链证据、AI 代理代理、智能合约争议和数字资产保全。

Features / 特点

Bilingual / 双语

All templates are provided in both English and Chinese (Simplified). Traditional Chinese versions are available upon request.

所有模板均提供英文和中文（简体）版本。繁体中文版本可应要求提供。

Digital World Adapted / 适配数字世界

  • DID Support: Decentralized Identifier fields for party identification / 去中心化身份标识字段
  • Blockchain Evidence: Structured fields for transaction hashes, smart contract addresses, and hash verification / 交易哈希、智能合约地址和哈希验证结构化字段
  • AI Agent Representation: Specific authorization framework for AI Agent participation / AI 代理参与的专门授权框架
  • Digital Asset Preservation: Token freeze, NFT preservation, smart contract pause measures / 代币冻结、NFT 保全、智能合约暂停措施
  • DAO Governance: Dispute resolution for DAO proposals, voting, and treasury management / DAO 提案、投票和金库管理的争议解决
