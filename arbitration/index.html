Platform Features

Paperless Filing

Submit claims, evidence, and documents entirely online. Our platform supports large file uploads with checksum verification.

Virtual Hearing

Participate in hearings from anywhere. Our integrated video platform supports evidence presentation, real-time transcription, and secure recording.

Secure & Confidential

End-to-end encryption for all communications. Role-based access control ensures only authorized parties can access case materials.
