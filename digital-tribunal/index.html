How It Works

Four Steps to Resolution

From case filing to final award, every step is digitized, transparent, and powered by AI assistance.

01
📝

Case Filing

AI-Assisted Submission

Submit your dispute online. AI automatically classifies the case type, extracts key claims, and generates a preliminary case summary for the tribunal.

  • ✓Smart form with auto-complete
  • ✓AI classifies dispute category
  • ✓Automatic evidence checklist
  • ✓Real-time filing status tracking
02
⚖️

Tribunal Formation

Intelligent Arbitrator Matching

AI recommends the most suitable arbitrators based on case type, jurisdiction, expertise, and language preferences. Parties confirm the panel.

  • ✓AI-powered arbitrator matching
  • ✓Expertise & jurisdiction filters
  • ✓Conflict-of-interest checks
  • ✓Party approval process
03
🎥

Virtual Hearing

Secure Digital Courtroom

Conduct hearings in a secure online room with real-time AI transcription, evidence presentation, and cross-examination — available 24/7 across time zones.

  • ✓Real-time AI transcription
  • ✓Digital evidence presentation
  • ✓Cross-examination tools
  • ✓Time-zone friendly scheduling
04
📜

Award Issuance

AI-Drafted, Human-Reviewed

AI assists in drafting the award based on hearing records and applicable law. The arbitrator reviews, modifies, and issues the final binding award.

  • ✓AI-assisted award drafting
  • ✓Legal precedent analysis
  • ✓Arbitrator review & approval
  • ✓Digital signature & enforcement
