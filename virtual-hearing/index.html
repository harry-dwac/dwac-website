Online Dispute Resolution

Virtual Hearing Room

A comprehensive online platform for conducting arbitration hearings — from initial filing to final award — entirely in a secure digital environment.

Platform

Hearing Capabilities

Enterprise-grade features designed for the demands of international arbitration proceedings.

Live Video Hearing

Real-time video proceedings connecting all participants — claimants, respondents, arbitrators, and expert witnesses — in a secure virtual courtroom environment.

Document Sharing

Secure evidence and document exchange with version control, access permissions, and a comprehensive audit trail. All submissions are encrypted and timestamped.

Real-time Transcription

Live text record of proceedings generated in real-time with multi-language support. Transcripts are searchable, exportable, and become part of the official case record.

Digital Signature

Secure electronic signing of awards, consent orders, and procedural documents. Compliant with international e-signature standards for legal validity.
