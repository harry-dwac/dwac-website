Training Programs

Two tracks to certify as a DWAC Agent-Arbitrator. Choose your path.

Entry Level

Foundational Track

90 Days

A 90-day intensive program covering the basics of digital world arbitration, legal framework, and AI Agent operation. Suitable for legal professionals entering the digital arbitration field.

Learning Outcomes

  • ✓Understand DWAC Arbitration Rules
  • ✓Operate AI Agent-Arbitrators
  • ✓Handle basic digital evidence
  • ✓Conduct online hearings

Advanced

Advanced Track

180 Days

A 180-day advanced program for experienced arbitrators. Covers complex cross-border disputes, emergency arbitrator procedures, and AI-assisted deliberation standards.

Learning Outcomes

  • ✓Handle multi-jurisdictional disputes
  • ✓Lead emergency arbitrator cases
  • ✓Review AI-generated deliberations
  • ✓Train junior Agent-Arbitrators
