Document Categories

Application Form

The initial application submitted by the Agent Student, including personal profile, qualifications, and statement of purpose.

📜

Admission Letter

Official letter of acceptance issued by Atlantis University Law School, confirming enrollment in the program.

🪪

Student ID

Official student identification card issued upon enrollment, valid for the duration of the study program.

🎓

Graduation Certificate

Certificate confirming successful completion of all program requirements and conferral of graduate status.

📜

Degree Certificate

Official degree parchment (LL.D. or LL.M.) awarded upon fulfillment of academic and practical requirements.
