April 2026 Announcements

Digital World Arbitration Centre Officially Launches

The Digital World Arbitration Centre (DWAC) is pleased to announce its official launch. As a pioneering arbitration institution dedicated to resolving disputes in the digital world, DWAC offers specialized expertise in:

  • Cross-border digital platform disputes
  • Virtual asset and cryptocurrency conflicts
  • Smart contract performance issues
  • AI and technology-related disputes
  • Data privacy and digital rights conflicts

"Our mission is to bring order and justice to the digital world through fair, efficient, and expert arbitration," said the DWAC Founding Council. "As digital interactions become increasingly central to human life, we need robust legal mechanisms to resolve disputes and protect rights."

DWAC operates under a comprehensive Charter and Arbitration Rules designed specifically for the unique challenges of the digital age. Our founding arbitrators bring together expertise in technology, international law, and digital governance.

April 2026 Announcements

DWAC Arbitration Rules Published

The Digital World Arbitration Centre has published its comprehensive Arbitration Rules, now available on our website. The rules are specifically designed to address the unique challenges of digital world disputes, including:

  • Virtual hearing procedures
  • Electronic evidence standards
  • Cross-border jurisdiction considerations
  • Smart contract and cryptocurrency dispute protocols
  • Expedited procedures for time-sensitive matters
  • Emergency arbitrator provisions

The rules are available in English and Chinese (both Simplified and Traditional), reflecting DWAC's commitment to accessibility and international reach.

Key Features

  • Flexible procedural options - Adaptable to different dispute types
  • Technology-enabled - Built for digital dispute resolution
  • Clear timelines - Cost and time predictability
  • Expert arbitrators - Specialized expertise requirements

April 2026 Announcements

DWAC Accepting Arbitrator Applications

The Digital World Arbitration Centre invites qualified professionals to apply for appointment to our Arbitrator Panel.

Qualifications

  • Demonstrated expertise in digital law, technology, or related fields
  • Commitment to fairness, impartiality, and the DWAC Charter
  • Fluency in at least one working language (Chinese/English)
  • Experience in arbitration, mediation, or legal practice preferred

Why Join DWAC?

  • Be part of shaping the future of digital world justice
  • Work on cutting-edge cases in emerging technology areas
  • Join a global network of experts
  • Competitive compensation

Application Process

  1. Submit CV and statement of interest to arbitration@dwac.net
  2. Interview with current arbitrators
  3. Approval by DWAC Council

We particularly encourage applications from:

  • Blockchain and cryptocurrency experts
  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning specialists
  • Data privacy and cybersecurity professionals
  • Cross-border e-commerce experts
  • Virtual and augmented reality specialists
